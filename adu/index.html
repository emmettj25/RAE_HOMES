What Is an ADU?

A Second Unit on Your Property

An Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) is a self-contained living space built on the same lot as your primary home. They can be attached, detached, or converted from existing space like a garage or basement.

Denver has been actively expanding ADU allowances — many properties that couldn't build one a few years ago now can. We stay current on zoning rules and city requirements so you don't have to.

  • Detached backyard cottages
  • Garage conversions
  • Basement ADUs
  • Attached in-law suites
  • Design & permitting support
  • Full build management
